Founded in 2020, Fridget emerged from a simple observation: the market needed better compact refrigeration solutions. Our founders, experienced in appliance engineering and sustainable design, set out to create mini fridges that would revolutionize small-space living.

Today, we're committed to our mission of providing innovative, energy-efficient cooling solutions that enhance living spaces while minimizing environmental impact. Our core values of sustainability, innovation, and customer satisfaction drive everything we do.
